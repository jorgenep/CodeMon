# CodeMon TCG 🎮🐛
### Turn your code into a collection.

CodeMon TCG is a local-first, lightweight VS Code extension that turns your coding grind into a nostalgic Trading Card Game experience. Earn XP by coding, crack open packs, and trade cards with secure link codes while keeping your data stored locally.

![CodeMon Marketplace Banner](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/YourUsername/codemon-tcg/main/resources/banner.png)

---

## 🚀 The Core Loop

### 🐛 Earn XP, Pull Packs
CodeMon rewards your workflow with a simple, local progression loop.
* **Manual Typing:** Earn XP for every new line you write.
* **Flow State:** Stay active over time to earn periodic Flow bonuses.
* **Bug Squashing:** Reducing diagnostics awards bonus XP.
* **The Pack Opening:** Spend XP on **Single Boosters**, **Blister Packs**, **Collector Tins**, **Elite Trainer Boxes**, and **Booster Boxes**.

### 🤝 Secure Clipboard Trading
Completed your set? Need that last piece?
* Generate a **Secure Trade Code** in-game.
* Copy it to your clipboard and share it with a friend.
* Paste the encrypted string into the trade view to import cards safely.
* No servers, no tracking—just local trade codes.

### 🎨 Binder & Shop
Use the Binder view to see your collection and open packs directly.
* The shop includes standard TCG-style pack formats.
* The binder updates after pack openings and trade imports.
* Your collection is stored locally in VS Code global state.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

**CodeMon TCG is built for the developer who values privacy.**
* **100% Local:** Your collection and state live on your machine, not on a server.
* **Secure Vault:** Your progress is stored in a simple encrypted vault format to discourage casual tampering.
* **No Telemetry:** We don't track your code, your projects, or your typing habits. Your data is yours.

---

## 🛠️ How to Get Started

1. **Install:** Install **CodeMon TCG** in VS Code.
2. **Code:** Keep an eye on the Status Bar. Your XP will naturally accumulate as you code.
3. **Shop:** Click the Status Bar balance to open your **Binder & Shop**.
4. **Trade:** Open the Trade tab to generate a link code or import one from a friend.
